HLTA: Houston Ladies Tennis Association

This league offers competitive ladies doubles matches on Monday mornings. There are divisions for all levels of play from beginners to advanced players.

The league operates a Fall season which runs from late August through late November or early December and a Spring season which begins in late January and runs through late April or early May. Byes are provided for Thanksgiving and Spring Break.

Boundaries of the league include most of Houston and surrounding areas. Teams are grouped into divisions based on their location to minimize travel times for away matches.

WHLTA: West Houston Ladies Tennis Association

This league offers competitive ladies doubles matches on Wednesday mornings. There are divisions for all levels of play from beginners to advanced players.

The league operates a Fall season which runs from late August through late November or early December and a Spring season which begins in late January and runs through late April or early May. Byes are provided for Thanksgiving and Spring Break.

Boundaries of the league include all of Houston and surrounding areas within these boundaries:

  • West of the 610 loop
  • South of 290
  • West as far as FM1463
  • South to the Sugarland area

KAT: Katy Area Tennis

This organization offers competitive men's, ladies', and mixed matches (leagues, ladders, and tournaments) on a variety of days and times. There are divisions for all levels of play from beginners to advanced players.

The organization operates a variety of leagues and ladders which run throughout the year. Tournaments are scheduled every quarter as well.
